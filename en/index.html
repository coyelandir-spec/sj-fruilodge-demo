Our philosophy

More than a place to stay — your gateway to Northern Madagascar

Set in Antsiranana, S&J FRUI'LODGE blends the comfort of a well-kept guesthouse with the region's spirit of adventure. Our local team welcomes you like friends and opens the doors to the finest treasures of the Far North.

From the Emerald Sea to the trails of Amber Mountain, from the Red Tsingy to the majestic Bay of Diego-Suarez, we craft every stay to suit you : serene, free and deeply authentic.
